...SAINT MARTIN HOTELS...



Hotel Kollol


This property is a 4-minute walk from the beach. Hotel Kollol offers accommodations in Saint Martin. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site. Every room includes a TV. Some units feature a sitting area for your convenience. Certain rooms have views of the sea or city. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. Extras include slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk and gift shop at the property. The hotel also provides car rental.
